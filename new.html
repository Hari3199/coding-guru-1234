Celebration of Rashtriya Ekta Diwas | National Unity Day 2023

Rashtriya Ekta Diwas – National Unity Day 2023 in commemoration of the 148th birth anniversary of Sri Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was celebrated by the Institute, on Tuesday, October 31, 2023 at 2:00 PM in the school Seminar Hall.

Observance Of Vigilance Awareness Week – 2023

In consonance with the directions of Central Vigilance Commission, Vigilance Awareness Week 2023 with its central theme “Say No to Corruption, commit to the Nation” was observed by IQAC of Canara College from October 26, 2023 to November 02, 2023.
