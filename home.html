Why choose Global Indian International School
Our global curricula delivered by outstanding faculty using world-class facilities and most awarded learning pedagogy- 9GEMS helps students aim for excellence. Rated ‘GOOD’ by the Department of Education and Knowledge (ADEK), the programmes of our Abu Dhabi Indian School are designed to not only impart academic skills but to groom students holistically.

Experience the GIIS Difference

Unique Learning Spaces for Early Years

Our Pre-KG and Kindergarten learning spaces include marketspace, gym, KG Library and Montessori Labs to impart all-rounded education from a young age.

Bridge Programme for effortless transition to Middle Years

The carefully analysed student-focused Bridge Programme is an effective way for Primary Grade students to be academically prepared for middle years.

Innovative programmes for university placements

Career Mapping and University Connect Programs initiated by GIIS help higher grade students to understand their potentiality, career-objective and prepare them to get enrolled in finest universities across the world.

Real- world technology exposure to develop futuristic skills

Global Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship, a department dedicated to the technological advancement of students from a young age.
